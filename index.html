About Me

As a PhD student at Case Western Reserve University, I am building upon my robust background in Machine Learning and AI from my MS in Computer Science at CWRU. My academic journey, initiated with a B.Tech, has been marked by intensive projects and research in critical areas like explainable AI and deep learning. Currently, my research focuses on Large Language Models (LLMs), specifically in biases, evaluation, and reasoning, while also exploring other facets of LLMs. My contributions have been recognized through awards and leadership positions. Proficient in a variety of programming languages and ML tools, I am committed to advancing the field of AI through innovative research and solutions. I am actively seeking internship opportunities to further deepen my knowledge and contribute to cutting-edge research.
