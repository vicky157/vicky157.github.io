Grammars of Formal Uncertainty

When to Trust LLMs in Automated Reasoning Tasks

Ganguly, D., Singh, V., Sankar, S., Zhang, B., Zhang, X., Iyengar, S., Han, X., Sharma, A., Kalyanaraman, S., Chaudhary, V.

NeurIPS 2025 • May 2025

This paper addresses the epistemological gap between probabilistic LLMs and deterministic formal verification by introducing a PCFG framework to model LLM outputs. We systematically evaluate five frontier LLMs, revealing domain-specific impacts of SMT-based autoformalization and demonstrating that task-dependent uncertainty signals enable selective verification with drastically reduced errors.

K⁴: Online Log Anomaly Detection

Via Unsupervised Typicality Learning

Singh, V.*, Chen, W.*, Rahmani, Z., Ganguly, D., Hariri, M., Chaudhary, V.

*Denotes equal contribution

HiPC 2025 • July 2025

K⁴ presents a novel online log anomaly detection framework based on unsupervised typicality learning. By modeling what is typical rather than what is anomalous, K⁴ achieves state-of-the-art performance in detecting novel anomalies without requiring labeled data, while maintaining low computational overhead suitable for production environments.

Efficient Fine-Grained GPU Performance Modeling

For Distributed Deep Learning of LLMs

Zhang, B., Zheng, M., Ganguly, D., Zhang, X., Singh, V., Chaudhary, V., Zhang, Z.

HiPC 2025 • September 2025

This work presents a fine-grained GPU performance modeling framework for distributed LLM training. By decomposing training into fundamental operations and modeling their characteristics at fine granularity, we achieve high prediction accuracy (under 10% error) while maintaining computational efficiency, enabling optimal parallelization strategy selection and hardware configuration.

Research Interests

  • Formal Reasoning and Verification: Developing rigorous formal logic methodologies, leveraging SMT‐LIB encodings and solver frameworks to verify, interpret, and enhance the correctness of LLM-generated reasoning.
  • Fine-Tuning LLMs and Vision Models: Leveraging techniques such as LoRA to optimize large language and vision models for specific tasks while maintaining computational efficiency.
  • Redundancy Mitigation in LLMs: Investigating approaches to reduce redundancy in large language models, enhancing performance and efficiency.
  • Model Optimization: Developing strategies for optimizing machine learning models, including pruning and hyperparameter tuning, to improve both accuracy and resource utilization.
  • Explainable AI: Advancing interpretability in AI models, focusing on enhancing transparency and providing actionable insights for users.
